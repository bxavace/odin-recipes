Best Burger Recipe

Description

This burger is a monster! Even though it can be prepared rather easily, no one can ignore the taste after the first bite!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 ounce of package dry onion soup mix
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 teaspoons of hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats

Steps:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion soup mix, egg, hot sauce, and oats. Shape into 6 patties.
  3. Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
